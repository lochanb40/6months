Small Yet Memorable Moments

Not every memory becomes a milestone. Some simply become impossible to forget.

The tiniest moments usually become the biggest memories. And somehow... these became some of my favourites.

📌
🌅

Our Good Mornings & Good Nights

Even before we started dating... Somehow every single day started with your "Good morning..." and ended with your "Good night." Looking back... I think we had already become each other's first thought every morning... and the last one every night.

📌
🏫

Waiting For Lunch

Every single school day... I waited for one thing. The bell after sixth period. Because I knew I'd finally get to see you. Those ordinary lunch breaks somehow became some of my favourite memories.

📌
🍫

Every Chocolate Was Yours

Every time I got a chocolate... The first thought was always... "I can't wait to give this to her." Maybe it was never really about the chocolate. It was just another excuse to be closer to you.

📌
🫶

My Protective Side

I still remember how protective I used to get around you. Every time someone came a little too close... I'd somehow find a way to pull you towards me or gently push them away. The thought of someone else bothering you annoyed me far more than it probably should have. And honestly... I think you secretly enjoyed watching me get jealous. Looking back now... It was just another way of saying, "I've got you."

📌
🌳

Our Little Spot By The Tree

Home time somehow became my favourite part of every school day. The moment we'd spot each other... It was like both our faces lit up. We'd stand near that tree talking about absolutely anything. Me teasing you. You pretending to be annoyed. Then I'd walk you all the way to your bus. I'd wait until it left... And only then would I turn around and walk home with the biggest smile on my face. Somehow... Even a few minutes with you were enough to make my whole day.

📌
👋

One Last Wave

Every single day... Whether I was inside school... Or standing near the bus stand... There was one thing I always waited for. Our wave. Before your bus disappeared... We'd always wave at each other. Sometimes you'd almost forget... Then suddenly turn back just to wave one last time. I'd stand there smiling long after you had gone. Such a tiny moment... Yet somehow the perfect way to say, "See you tomorrow."

📌
👀

The Eye Contact & Random Touches

Out of every little memory... This might be my favourite. Our random eye contact. Whether during assembly... Or while secretly looking at each other... The second our eyes met... We'd both smile... Blush... And immediately look away like complete idiots. Then there were those random little touches. Hands brushing together. Shoulders bumping. Tiny moments that somehow made my heart race every single time. I think those little moments made me fall for you even more.

📌
💬

When I Made You Blush

I loved teasing you on chat. I'd randomly flirt with you... Call you "Jaanu" or "Señorita"... And within seconds... Your mind would completely stop working. Sometimes you'd take forever to reply. Sometimes you'd send the cutest flustered messages. I had way too much fun making you blush. But now... Look at us. You've somehow completely turned the tables. These days... You're the one making me blush like an idiot.

📌
🚗

Our Little Online Dates

Even before we officially started dating... We somehow found our own little way of going on dates. I'd pick you up in my imaginary car or bike. We'd visit parks. Walk together. Sit beside each other. Rest against one another. We acted more like a couple than most actual couples... Yet somehow we still called ourselves best friends. One day... I can't wait to turn every one of those imaginary moments into real ones.

📌
📸

Stealing The Goofiest Pictures

We've been doing this since the very first day we met. Somehow we both made it our mission to collect the goofiest pictures of each other. Every funny face... Every random selfie... Every blurry picture... Somehow became another memory. You still keep saying you have more embarrassing pictures of me... But let's be honest... I'm pretty sure you don't even have fifty proper ones, do you my love? Those silly little pictures mean more to me than you'll ever know.

If I had to explain why I love you...

We'd be here forever.

But let's start with our story. ❤️
