Our Story

Every beautiful story has a beginning. Ours started with one random conversation... and somehow became my favourite story.

Every memory. Every laugh. Every little moment. They all led us here.

🌸
25 October 2025

Our First Conversation

It was in your old classroom that I met you for the very first time. Looking back now, it's crazy how everything started from such a random little conversation. We talked for a while, and before I even realised it, we had exchanged our Instagram IDs. At the time, it didn't feel like anything special. It just felt like another conversation. When I got home, I kept staring at your profile, wondering if I should text you or not. I hesitated so much because I kept thinking, "What if she thinks I'm weird?" or "What if she feels awkward?" But somehow, I gathered the courage to send that first text. And honestly... I'm so glad I did. Because that one tiny message became the start of every beautiful memory we've made together. ❤️

"Sometimes the smallest conversations end up changing your entire life."

One Random Day ❤️

The Challenge I Lost On Purpose

It was just another random day where we couldn't stop laughing over the stupidest things. Somehow, we ended up talking about my NCC certificate receiving ceremony, and I told you how hard it would be for me to keep a straight face during the entire event. That's when we made one of the dumbest little deals ever. If I laughed or even smiled once during the ceremony, you could do absolutely anything you wanted to my hair. But if I managed to survive without smiling... I would get to pinch your cheeks. I still remember how I used to tease you by saying, "You'll only win if I let you." Little did you know... I had already decided I was going to lose. Not because I couldn't control my smile... But because I wanted to see your smile. Watching you get all excited after winning was worth it. I still remember you standing so close to me, carefully tying those tiny little ponytails in my hair with the biggest smile on your face. It was probably the only time I was genuinely happy that I had lost. And if I had to do it all over again... I'd lose that challenge every single time. ❤️

"Sometimes losing becomes the best victory."

A Day I'll Never Forget

Our First Hug

I still remember how nervous we both were that day... Especially you. The walk behind the auditorium somehow felt longer than it actually was because neither of us really knew what to do or what to say. Then came our awkward little bracelet exchange. And then... We hugged. There was no awkward silence. No uncomfortable feeling. Just the two of us standing there, holding each other, while our hearts were beating faster than ever. I still remember gently brushing your hair back and telling you that everything was going to be okay. Then, of course... Eeva appeared out of nowhere. Everything instantly became awkward, I panicked, and I literally ran away. But after she left... We hugged again. Even today... I can still remember exactly how that hug felt.

"Some hugs last seconds... but stay forever."

1 February 2026

The First Peck 💋

I still remember waiting outside your classroom after my Economics exam. The second the bell rang, I practically ran there just hoping to see you. Then you came... My heart genuinely forgot how to function. We went backstage again. I gave you your card. Your pendant. We hugged tighter than ever before. You started crying... I remember wiping your tears, resting my forehead against yours, trying to tell you everything would be okay without saying much at all. Then our stupid little dance... The photos that definitely didn't look like "best friend" photos. And then... That tiny little peck. It only lasted a second... But I don't think I'll ever forget how happy it made me.

"One tiny kiss... one unforgettable memory."

Children's Day

One Perfect Day 🌸

This day... Honestly became one of my favourite memories. We looked amazing together. I don't think I've ever looked away from you that entire assembly. I was so nervous when I came to talk to you. I even asked everyone to leave just so I could spend a little more time with you. Then... I fell like an idiot. And before anyone else could help... You were already beside me. You cleaned my hands. You kept asking if I was okay. I pretended I couldn't do some things myself... Just because I wanted you to stay close for a little longer. I didn't care about the pain. My heart was already too busy beating because of you.

"Sometimes falling down lets you realize who'll always be there to lift you up."

31 January 2026

Our First Date ❤️

Where do I even begin... Days before we met, I bought a whole new outfit just so I could match yours. The night before, I barely slept. I had flowers ready. Everything was planned. Then you arrived... Wearing that blue dress... And my pendant. My mind went completely blank. From holding your hand... To walking together... Sharing ice cream... Making silly vlogs... Laughing like two complete idiots... Everything felt so natural. Then came the park. Holding your waist. Our accidental lip kiss. Running away because someone almost caught us. Laughing even harder. And finally... Our very first real kiss. Just you. Just me. Just one moment that changed everything. Even today... I still smile every time I think about it.

"The day two best friends accidentally became something much more."
