20 Little Things I Love About You

Some reasons are big. Some are tiny. But every single one is true.

01

Your annoyingly cute smile.

The kind of smile that melts my heart every single time I see it. I don't think you'll ever understand just how much I love seeing you smile.

02

Your beautiful eyes.

Those cute eyes somehow manage to pull me into a little world where it's just you and me. I could honestly stare at them forever.

03

Your real smile.

Not the smile you show everyone else... The smile you show when you're with me. That smile means everything to me.

04

Your goofy little laugh.

Your silly, kid-like laugh is honestly one of the cutest things I've ever heard. It makes even my worst days better.

05

The way you act like a kid.

Your dramatic little moments. Your childish behaviour. Your random chaos. Every bit of it is unbelievably adorable.

06

Your strength.

No matter how difficult life gets... No matter how many times you feel like giving up... You never do. You keep going. And that's one of the things I admire most about you.

07

The way you look at me.

I don't know if you even notice it... But every time you look at me, you somehow make me feel like I'm the luckiest person alive.

08

The way you always try to comfort me.

Even when you don't know exactly what to say... You still try. You never stop trying to make me feel better. And that means more than you know.

09

Your good mornings.

"Heyyyy babeeeeee... Gooddddd morningggggg." I don't think you realise how much those little messages brighten my day. They've become my favourite way to start every morning.

10

Your voice notes.

I could honestly listen to them for hours. Your voice somehow manages to calm me down, make me smile, and make me miss you all at the same time.

11

Your endless yapping.

I honestly never get tired of listening to you. Whether it's your random stories, your silly little made-up adventures, or you just talking about your day... I could listen to you forever.

12

The real you.

I love that you can be completely yourself around me. The goofy, chaotic, dramatic, free version of you... is my favourite version of you.

13

Your innocence.

Sometimes the things you say, the way you react, or the way you get excited... are just so unbelievably innocent. It's honestly one of the cutest things about you.

14

The way you always surprise me.

You somehow manage to catch me off guard every single time. Just when I think I know everything about you... you surprise me again. And I absolutely love that.

15

Your bossy side.

You don't do it all the time... But whenever you suddenly become all bossy and confident... I can't help but smile. I don't know why... I just love that side of you.

16

The way you always want to be close to me.

Whether it's holding my hand, hugging me, or simply wanting to spend time together... You always make me feel loved. And honestly... I never want that feeling to end.

17

The way you talk about our future.

You don't just talk about it... You make it feel real. Every little dream, every silly plan, every "one day..." Somehow you make me believe that we'll get there together.

18

The way you are with kids.

Seeing you with children is honestly one of the cutest things I've ever seen. The way you smile, the way you care, the way you play with them... It melts my heart every single time.

19

Your kindness.

You always want to help people. Even the ones who have hurt you. I may not always understand it... But I love how big your heart is. Never lose that part of yourself.

20

The way you never gave up on us.

No matter how many fights... No matter how many arguments... No matter how many moments where we weren't even sure what was going to happen... You stayed. You chose us. And I'll never stop being grateful for that. I love you. Thank you for never giving up on us. ❤️

And honestly...

These are only twenty little things. If I tried to write down every single reason I love you... I'd probably never finish. Because every single day, you give me another reason to fall in love with you all over again. ❤️
